PROJECTS

COBBLE

Lead Designer | Product Owner

Cobble is a 2.5D side scrolling puzzle-adventure game created in the short time span of 48 hours for the 2020 Global Game Jam. Afterward, our team of 8 students continued development on the project in the pre-production phase for a few months.

My Contributions

  • - Responsible for all documents, level design, and systems design
  • - Built levels, environments, lighting, and adjusted systems in UE4
  • - Managed team using Agile Scrum development methodologies
  • - Scheduled and ran all team meetings both on site and remotely
  • - Lead and maintained the creative direction among a large development team

NEO

Solo Project

NEO is a prototype for a dungeon crawler style game with an emphasis on movement and evasion. I developed this game in GamemakerStudio2 for a semester long project, which had an emphasis on overall game design, game feel, and exploring unfamiliar dev tools. Consequently, I programmed a custom particle system to handle all the VFX in Game Maker Language.

FADE

Lead Designer | Lead Artist

Fade is a short atmospheric puzzle game that takes place in a mysterious, eerie world. This game was developed in 5 months for the SkillsUSA Game Competition. Fade won 1st place at the state Level, and at the national level placed 7th.

My Contributions

  • - Rigged and Animated Character in Maya
  • - 3D Modeled assets for the environment
  • - Programmed systems in Unreal Engine 4 Blueprints
  • - Designed levels and built environments
  • - Pitched and showcased game to industry professionals

Luminescent

Lead Designer | Lead Programmer

Luminescent is a 2D side scrolling Metroidvania that uses drives players to explore a dark corrupted ocean. More recent than FADE, this game was also developed across a team of 3 people (myself included) in a total of 5 months for the SkillsUSA Game Competition. Luminescent won 1st place at the state Level, and at the national level placed 5th.

My Contributions

  • - Designed all gameplay systems, levels, and encounters
  • - Built environments, animated enemies, and created VFX in Unity
  • - Programmed all systems and encounters in C#
  • - Established the game's art style and lead the creative direction
  • - Pitched and showcased game to industry professionals

ABOUT ME

I'm an undergraduate game design student at Champlain College anticipating graduation in 2023. I'm also minoring in game programming to become more proficient in the technical side of games. I love working with others and collaborating on projects everyone is mutually passionate about. Constantly, I'm striving to learn from new experiences and seek out unfamiliar territory where I can deliver beyond expectations.

I'm all about crafting an experience, and games are an extremely prevalent medium others can interact with. Designing and creating content for players to experience excites and drives me to invest all of my efforts toward challenging myself to improve as a developer and designer. Outside of work, school, and my personal game projects, I like to spend my time outdoors. Whenever I have the chance, I'm hiking, skiing, snowboarding, or rock climbing.
