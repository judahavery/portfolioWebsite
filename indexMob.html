Play Spring Fever

itch.io Page

Spring Fever is a game I created solo for the 2020 Unreal Spring Jam in under 5 days. The jam's theme was: "What Is Hidden In Snow Comes Forth In The Thaw." I adapted that idea to a puzzle game where each time you move, the environment around you and your character gradually melt. Play as a snowman trying to escape the treacherous spring heat!

Play Cobble

Game Jam itch.io

Cobble is a 2.5D puzzle-platformer taking place in an industrial steampunk world. Manipulate the mechanics of your environment with your clocksmith abilities to traverse an enormous clocktower filled with different obstacles and mysteries. The base game was created in under 48 hours for the 2020 Global Game Jam. After the jam, our team of 8 students worked in pre-production on COBBLE for 3 months.

Play NEO

itch.io Page

NEO is a prototype for a top-down "Beat 'em Up" with an emphasis on fast-paced movement in combat. Throw and retrieve your axe while evading enemies and obstacles. After being dropped into a maze of dangerous rooms, your goal is to fight your way to the trophy room, completing the level. NEO was created for a game design class from my fall 2019 semester.

FADE

Lead Designer | Lead Artist

Systems
Level Design
Art / Animation
Programming

Watch Gameplay

Fade is a short atmospheric puzzle game that takes place in a mysterious, eerie world. With a focus on atmosphere, most of my work on FADE took place in level design, 3D art/animation, and environmental storytelling. The game was developed in 5 months for the SkillsUSA Game Competition. Fade won 1st place at the state level, and at the national level placed 7th.
