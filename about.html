About Me

My name's Judah Avery, I'm currently attending Champlain College and expected to graduate in 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Game Design. During my high school experience, I worked across the full spectrum of game development. I 3D modeled, rigged, animated, and programmed on the technical side along with designing the levels and systems for my game projects. At Champlain College, I'm continuing to develop those skills, but I'm also working in larger teams on development projects learning how to manage workflow within a professional environment.

I'm truly passionate about crafting experiences and games are an extremely effective form of interaction. The thought of creating content that others will experience excites and drives me to create games.
