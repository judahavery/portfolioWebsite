Project Overview

COBBLE is a 2.5D side scrolling puzzle-adventure game. The base game was created in under 48 hours for the 2020 Global Game Jam, and afterward, our team of 8 students continued to develop the project further. Cobble began pre-production and I acted as product owner and lead designer. Development continued for a few months until the team was no longer able to continue working on the game. Most gameplay systems were implemented, all design documents, pipelines, and production documents were completed, and many art assets were created towards the end. Even though the progress we made over these 3 months didn't take place in the amount of game content, I gained a lot from COBBLE.
