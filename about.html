About Me

My name's Judah Avery, I'm an undergraduate Computer Science student at Champlain College expected to graduate in 2023. I'm extremely passionate about crafting unique worlds and experiences for others to enjoy. I found my passion during my third year of high school when I switched to a tech school. Through the Computer Animation and Web Design program I covered a lot of different territory. In the program, I worked on projects in web development, graphic design, animation, and game development. After my first attempt at a small game project, I became obsessed. All of my focus transitioned to learning programming, 3D art, and explored game engines. Outside of work, school, and my personal game development projects I like to spend my time outdoors. Whenever I have the opportunity, I'm hiking, skiing, snowboarding, or rock climbing.
